dcplaya is a free music player for the dreamcast.
<p>
It can play most popular music file formats such as mp3, ogg-vorbis, most
tracker file formats (xm,mod,s3m...) and some old school soundchip formats
like sc68 and sid.
<p>
Other formats can be easily added by third party developper without changing
dcplaya version.  
<p>
dcplaya has many other features such as image file handling,
visual effects ...<b>
Read the <a href="">features</a> for more info.
